求积分
$$
\huge{\int_0^\pi\frac{x\sin x}{1-2r\cos x+r^2}\mathrm dx}
$$
$$
\huge{=\frac{1}{2}\int_{-\pi}^\pi\frac{x\sin x}{1-2r\cos x+r^2}\mathrm dx}
$$
$$
\huge{=\frac{1}{4i}\int_{-\pi}^\pi\frac{x(e^{ix}-e^{-ix})}{(r-e^{ix})(r-e^{-ix})}\mathrm dx}
$$
$$
\huge{=\frac{1}{2i}\int_{-\pi}^\pi\frac{xe^{ix}}{(r-e^{ix})(r-e^{-ix})}\mathrm dx}
$$
令$\displaystyle z=e^{ix}则\mathrm dx = \frac{1}{iz}\mathrm dz$
得到
$$
\huge{=-\frac{1}{2i}\oint\frac{\ln z}{(r-z)(r-\frac{1}{z})}\mathrm dz}
$$
$$
\huge{=-\pi Res[\frac{\ln z}{(r-z)(r-\frac{1}{z})}]}=\frac{\pi r\ln r}{|r^2-1|}
$$
