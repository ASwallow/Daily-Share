 求定积分
 $$
 \huge\int_0^\pi\frac{\pi+\cos x}{x^2-\pi x+2026}\mathrm dx
 $$
 令$x-\dfrac{\pi}{2}=t$
 得到
 $$
 \huge\int_{-\frac{\pi}{2}}^\frac{\pi}{2}\frac{\pi-\sin t}{t^2-\frac{\pi^2}{4}+2026}\mathrm dt
 $$
 $$
 \huge =2\pi\int_0^\frac{\pi}{2}\frac{\mathrm dt}{t^2-\frac{\pi^2}{4}+2026}
 $$
 $$
 \huge =\frac{2\pi}{\sqrt{2026-\frac{\pi^2}{4}}}(\arctan\frac{\pi}{2\sqrt{2026-\frac{\pi^2}{4}}})
 $$
