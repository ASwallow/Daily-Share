解方程
$$
\huge\frac{\mathrm dy}{\mathrm dx}=\frac{1+xy^3}{1+x^3y}
$$
$$
\huge(1+xy^3)\mathrm dx+(1+x^3y)\mathrm dy=0
$$
令$u=x+y,v=xy$
关键在于$\mathrm du=\mathrm dx+\mathrm dy,\mathrm dv=x\mathrm dy+y\mathrm dx$
一个关键的等式：
$$
\huge\mathrm d(\frac{u}{v})=-\frac{1}{x^2}\mathrm dx-\frac{1}{y^2}\mathrm dy
$$
$$
\huge -v^2\mathrm d(\frac{u}{v})=x^2\mathrm dy+y^2\mathrm dx
$$
所以
$$
\huge \mathrm du-uv^2\mathrm d(\frac{u}{v})=0
$$
$$
\huge(1-uv)\mathrm du+u^2\mathrm dv=0
$$
$$
\huge \frac{\mathrm dv}{\mathrm du}-\frac{v}{u}=-\frac{1}{u^2}
$$
$$
\huge u\frac{\mathrm d\dfrac{v}{u}}{\mathrm du}=-\frac{1}{u^2}
$$
$$
\huge v=\frac{1}{2u}+Cu
$$
$$
\huge xy(x+y)=\frac{1}{2}+C(x+y)^2
$$
