解方程
$$
\huge\frac{\mathrm dy}{\mathrm dx}=\frac{y}{x^6y^3-x}
$$
$$
\huge \Rightarrow\frac{\mathrm dx}{\mathrm dy}=\frac{1}{y}(x^6y^3-x)
$$
$$
\huge \Rightarrow\frac{\mathrm dx}{x}=\frac{\mathrm dy}{y}(x^5y^3-1)
$$
令$\huge s=\ln x,t =\ln y$
$$
\huge\Rightarrow \mathrm ds=\mathrm dt(e^{5s+3t}-1)
$$
令$\huge 5s+3t=u$
$$
\huge \Rightarrow\mathrm du=\mathrm dt(5e^u-2)
$$
$$
\huge \Rightarrow \frac{1}{2}\ln(5-2e^{-5s-3t})+C=t
$$
$$
\huge\Rightarrow \frac{1}{2}\ln(5-\frac{2}{x^5y^3})+C=\ln y
$$
