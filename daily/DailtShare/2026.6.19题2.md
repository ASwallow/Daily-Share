求极限

$$
\huge{\lim_{n\rightarrow+\infty}n^3\int_0^n\frac{\displaystyle (1-\cos\frac{t}{n})\ln(1+\frac{t}{n})}{t^3(1+t^2)}\mathrm dt}
$$

由$Taylor展开$,$\displaystyle 1-\cos\frac{t}{n}=(\frac{t}{n})^2+o((\frac{t}{n})^2)$，$\displaystyle \ln(1+\frac{t}{n})=\frac{t}{n}+o(\frac{t}{n})$

得到
$$
\huge{\lim_{n\rightarrow+\infty}n^3\int_0^n\frac{\displaystyle (1-\cos\frac{t}{n})\ln(1+\frac{t}{n})}{t^3(1+t^2)}\mathrm dt}
$$
$$
\huge{=\lim_{n\rightarrow+\infty}\int_0^n\frac{1+o(1)}{1+t^2}\mathrm dt}
$$
$$
\huge{=\frac{\pi}{2}+\lim_{n\rightarrow+\infty}\int_0^n\frac{o(1)}{1+t^2}\mathrm dt}
$$
取一个$N$使得$n>N时o(1)<\varepsilon且m>n>N$
则
$$
\huge{\int_0^m-\int_0^n\frac{o(1)}{1+t^2}\mathrm dt=\int_n^m\frac{o(1)}{1+t^2}\mathrm dt<\frac{\pi}{2}\varepsilon}
$$
由柯西审敛法知道第二项为0
从而原极限为$\displaystyle\frac{\pi}{2}$
