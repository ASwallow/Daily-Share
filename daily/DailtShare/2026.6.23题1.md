$$
\huge \frac{\mathrm dy}{\mathrm dx}=\frac{1}{x\sin^2xy}-\frac{y}{x}
$$
令$t=xy,\dfrac{\mathrm dt}{\mathrm dx}=y+x\dfrac{\mathrm dy}{\mathrm dx}$
化简得到
$$
\huge \dfrac{\dfrac{\mathrm dt}{\mathrm dx}-y}{x}=\frac{1}{x\sin^2xy}-\frac{y}{x}
$$
$$
\huge \sin^2t\mathrm dt=\mathrm dx 
$$
$$
\huge \frac{xy}{2}-\frac{1}{4}\sin^2xy=x+C
