求级数
$$
\huge \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{\sin\frac{k\pi}{n}}{n+\frac{1}{k}}
$$
首先放大
$$
\huge \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{\sin\frac{k\pi}{n}}{n+\frac{1}{k}}<\huge \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^n\sin\frac{k\pi}{n}
$$
$$
\huge = \int_0^1\sin \pi x\mathrm dx=\frac{2}{\pi}
$$
再缩小
$$
\huge \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{\sin\frac{k\pi}{n}}{n+\frac{1}{k}}>\huge \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac{1}{n+1}\sum_{k=1}^n\sin\frac{k\pi}{n}
$$
$$
\huge = \huge \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\frac{n}{n+1}\cdot\frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^n\sin\frac{k\pi}{n}
$$
$$
\huge = \int_0^1\sin \pi x\mathrm dx=\frac{2}{\pi}
$$
由夹逼定理
$$
\huge \lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{\sin\frac{k\pi}{n}}{n+\frac{1}{k}}=\frac{2}{\pi}
$$
